Health Professions Conference Concept

The Health Professions Conference is the first students-based conference accredited by the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties. It is one of the major annual programs held at King Saud Bin Abdulaziz University for Health Sciences that is planned, organized, and executed by the studentsâ€™ club under the supervision of deanship of students affairs. The Fifth Health Professions Conference offers multiple lectures and various workshops that interest health practitioners. Selection of the best researches are also presented competing for the top positions.

What is HPC 2020?

The 5th Health Professions Conference (HPC) aims to discuss the National Transformation Program 2020. The program seeks to identify the challenges facing governmental agencies and construct the necessary institutional capacity for realizing the ambitious goals of Vision 2030. Our objective in HPC 2020 is to highlight the main core of Health Sector National Transformation Program.
