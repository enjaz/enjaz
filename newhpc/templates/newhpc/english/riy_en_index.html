Health Professions Conference Concept

The Health Professions Conference is the first students-based conference accredited by the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties. It is one of the major annual programs held at King Saud Bin Abdulaziz University for Health Sciences that is planned, organized, and executed by the students’ club under the supervision of deanship of students affairs. The Fifth Health Professions Conference offers multiple lectures and various workshops that interest health practitioners. Selection of the best researches are also presented competing for the top positions.
