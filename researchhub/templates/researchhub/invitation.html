We are very happy to invite you to be part of the research opportunity program, ResearchHub! ResearchHub is a platform to facilitate the process of doing research between KSAU-HS students and doctors.

This program aims to organize the process of finding a primary investigator for a student project, recruiting a team, asking for help in specific areas of research such as data collection and analysis, and it also allows doctors to reach a large number of students in case said doctor wants students to join in on a research project, or even in cases where students can help with gathering data, or to do any other task that is needed.

By signing-up, a profile will be created for you on ResearchHub, and it will be managed by our team. No changes will be done to your profile without your knowledge, and we will ask for updates regularly via email.
